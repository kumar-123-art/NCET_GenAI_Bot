Exciting: Prabhas signs multi-film deal with Salaar makers

Published on Nov 8, 2024 2:00 PM IST
Join Us Follow Us Add as a preferred
source on Google
3.4K

Prabhas continues to make waves in Tollywood, standing as the only star with over five projects simultaneously in various stages of production. Fresh off the success of Salaar and Kalki 2898 AD, the actor has now expanded his already impressive lineup by signing two additional films.

Hombale Films, the production house behind Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire and its forthcoming sequel Salaar: Part 2 – Shouryanga Parvam, has solidified its collaboration with Prabhas through a three-movie agreement. Although specific details about the projects are still under wraps, it is confirmed that Salaar 2 is part of this deal, while the other two films are set to release in 2027 and 2028.

This announcement comes as a firm response to dispel any rumours about a rift between Hombale Films and Prabhas following Salaar’s performance at the box office. The strengthened partnership makes it clear that both the actor and the production house are committed to future ventures together, putting any speculations to rest.

Industry sources suggest that one of Prabhas’ upcoming films may be directed by Prasanth Varma, with another anticipated project with acclaimed filmmaker Lokesh Kanagaraj. Though unconfirmed, these rumours have heightened excitement among fans eager to see Prabhas’ continued evolution in new and compelling roles.

Stay tuned for updates on these highly anticipated projects, as Prabhas and Hombale Films promise to bring more cinematic spectacles to the screen.
